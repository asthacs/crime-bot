This website is a tool to help users efficiently register for a crime using a chatbot. Our mission is to make every user comfortable and confident with registering their complaints, and providing them a safe space to do so. We also aim to spread awareness to all users about crimes, their rights, and other such common questions.

Our team has ensured that the chatbot is easy to use, extremely user-friendly, and convenient. All the complaints registered are directed to the nearest police station for further investigation. The users are regularly updated via email about their complaint.
